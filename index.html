What is KidniFy?

Discover KidniFy, the ultimate kidney health app. KidniFy combines machine learning, deep learning, and IoT to offer early CKD intervention. Assess your CKD risk, track your health stage, analyze medical images, get personalized diet plans, and check water quality with KidnyPure - all in one app. Take control of your kidney health and enjoy a healthier life with KidniFy.
